What RSO Actually Is

RSO (Rick Simpson Oil) is a thick, concentrated cannabis extract made using whole-plant material. Beginners struggle because it’s sticky, temperature-sensitive, and hard to measure. This roadmap gives you the simple, mechanical steps to start safely and avoid common mistakes.

How to Start Safely

  • Start tiny: Begin with a grain-of-rice sized amount.
  • Go slow: Wait at least 2 hours before taking more.
  • Stay consistent: Use the same amount for 3–4 days before increasing.
  • Use the right tools: Most beginner problems come from using the wrong capsules or funnels.
